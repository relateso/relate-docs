## Overview
Relate is available as a desktop application and from the browser.

We recommend accessing Relate via desktop application for the most optimal experience.

## Desktop app
Relate is available for macOS Intel, macOS Apple Silicon, and Windows. Download the app at [download.relate.so](https://download.relate.so).

## In browser app
Go to [app.relate.so](https://app.relate.so) to sign in/up.

Most features and layouts will render in browser. Relate should work on most browsers, however, we recommend using Google Chrome for best experience.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do you support Linux?">
    Officially, we do not. However, if you require running Relate on Linux, reach out to us and we can send a Linux install app.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Will you build a mobile/table app?">
    Yes. However, we don't have a timeline set for building it. Our focus is on creating the best experience for B2B teams to track sales and customer relationships from the desktop.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I quit Relate?">
    By default, hitting `⌘+Q` (or `Ctrl+Q`) will minimize the app to tray. To quit completely, hit `⌥+⌘+Q`).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
