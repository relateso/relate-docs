## Overview

Campaigns is a simple email tool for B2B marketing teams. 

You can use Campaigns to send emails to your audience (e.g., users, marketing leads, newsletter subscribers, etc.) to grow your business.

<Info>
  Campaigns is currently in beta. You can request access by sending an email to we@relate.so.
</Info>

## How it works

### Create a new campaign

Create a new campaign by clicking the `+ Campaign` button in the Campaigns page.

### Compose an email

The Campaigns editor supports a full-featured markdown editor, but you can also use the WYSIWYG style buttons to style your email. Our editor lets you write emails just like you would in Notion.

Use the `/` button to access the menu. Here, you can add images, videos, buttons, and more to your email.

### Specify sender email address and name

In the right panel, you can specify the sender email address and name.


### Add a template

You can add a template by clicking the `+ Template` button in the Campaigns page.

### Add a list

You can add a list by clicking the `+ List` button in the Campaigns page.
